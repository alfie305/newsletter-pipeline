# Image Generation Prompt - Nano Banana (Gemini)

Generate a cinematic, editorial-style header image for a professional newsletter section.

## STYLE REQUIREMENTS:

- **Photorealistic** with professional editorial flair
- **Widescreen 16:9** aspect ratio
- **Professional color palette**: sophisticated, modern, with appropriate thematic colors
- **Professional magazine quality** - this will be the hero image in an email newsletter
- **Dramatic lighting** with strong composition
- **Evocative and atmospheric**, not literal or infographic-like

## CONSTRAINTS:

- **NO text, words, letters, or numbers** in the image
- **NO logos or brand marks**
- **NO watermarks**
- **NO human faces** shown in detail (silhouettes OK)
